Afterschools Playclub

Afterschools

Bulrush Nursery

Senior Citizens

Our Afterschools Playclub aims to provide a wide range of activities for children aged 4-11. At an affordable price you can arrange for your child to be picked up from school and to be looked after until 6.00pm.

Activites include

  • Free play
  • Arts and Crafts
  • Dancing
  • Monkeynastics
  • African Dancing
  • Drama
  • Trips during school holidays

If you would like more information or to arrange a visit please contact Tracey Whittley on 9035 1020 or call into the Vine at 193 Crumlin Road.
