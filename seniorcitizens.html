Senior Citizens

Our Senior Citizens Lunch Club runs every Thursday from 1:00pm - 3:00pm. It is open to anyone to anyone living in the area who wants to get out of the house and spend an enjoyable time in the company of others for a couple of hours each week.


Lunch is served at 1:00pm every Thursday and consists of two courses, tea/coffee and biscuits at a cost of Â£3.00.


As well as lunch, there is a programme of activity in the afternoon which offers something for everyone, including talks from invited speakers, arts and crafts activities and a little light exercise for those who want to stay fit and active.
