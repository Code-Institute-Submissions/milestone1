Advice Services

The Vine Centre has been providing advice in the Crumlin Road area for 38 years. We can provide free, confidential advice on the following:

  • Income Support
  • Social Fund
  • Disability Living Allowance
  • Incapacity Benefit
  • Attendance Allowance
  • Employment
  • Housing
  • Housing Benefit
  • Debt
  • Tax Credits
  • Consumer Issues

If you feel you need support please contact us.

We can make telephone calls, write and reply to letters and fill in forms on your behalf. we can also offer support in terms of preparation for representation at Tribunals and Appeals, as well as access to a dedicated money advice worker.

The Vine Centre's advice staff are experienced trained advice professionals who can provide you with expert assistance. No request for advice or assistance is too small or too much trouble.
