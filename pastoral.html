Pastoral Care

The Pastoral support at the Vine Centre is a non-judgmental service of: befriending, listening, encouraging and support to promote individual wellbeing.

At times in our lives we find ourselves in situations where we would need someone perhaps other than a family member or a firend that we can turn to. Someone to be there in times of loneliness, someone to listen, someone to encourage us through difficulties, and someone to guide us in the right direction to get advice or professional help.

If you feel you need support please contact us.

Contact Us

Pastoral support is for the lonely, the bereaved, families dealing with difficult situations, those caring for others, those who are ill, and people who find themselves confined to their home for one reason or another. In many ways the list is endless but if you feel you or someone you know would benefit from this service please do not hesitate to contact our pastoral support worker.

David Surgenor

Pastoral Support Worker

Our Pastoral Support Worker will be happy to meet you in your home, or if you prefer at the Vine Centre where you be sure you can speak to the pastoral support privately and confidentially.
